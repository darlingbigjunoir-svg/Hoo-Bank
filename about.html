OUR STORY & MISSION

Building the Future
of Digital Finance

HooBank was founded with a single belief: managing money should be effortless, secure, and accessible to everyone — from first-time savers to global enterprises.

Our Mission

To democratise access to next-generation payment infrastructure, empowering individuals and businesses with the tools they need to thrive in a cashless world.

Our Vision

A world where every transaction is instant, borderless, and completely secure — where financial opportunity is not defined by geography or circumstance.

Our Values

Trust, transparency, and relentless innovation guide every product decision we make. We put our users first — always.

2017

The Idea

Three engineers frustrated by slow cross-border payments sketched out HooBank on a whiteboard in San Francisco.

2018

Seed Round

Raised $4.2M in seed funding and launched a private beta with 500 early adopters.

2020

Series A & Global Launch

$28M Series A fuelled expansion into 40 countries and our first enterprise partnerships.

Today

3800+ Users & Growing

Processing $230M+ in transactions monthly across 80 countries with industry-leading security.

A story driven by
purpose, not just profit.

What started as a weekend hack to split rent between roommates evolved into one of the fastest-growing fintech platforms in the world. We refused to accept that moving money had to be complicated, expensive, or exclusive.

Today, HooBank powers payments for freelancers in Lagos, SMBs in Berlin, and enterprise teams across Singapore — all from the same beautifully simple platform.
